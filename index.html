About Black Terra Energies Ltd

Excellence, Integrity, and Innovation in Energy Solutions

Established in 2024, Black Terra Energies Ltd is a dynamic Nigerian energy company committed to delivering reliable oil, gas, and petroleum solutions. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, we are strategically positioned to serve clients across Nigeria and West Africa.

Our company was founded on the principles of excellence, integrity, and innovation. We understand the critical role that reliable energy supply plays in Nigerian economic development and are committed to contributing to Nigeria's energy security and sustainable growth.
